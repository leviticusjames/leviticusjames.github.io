Who is This Guy?

Story is how I make sense of my world—how I deconstruct it, experience it, and share it. Writing is how I join the song that story sings. It’s my passion. My contribution. Whether it’s a spark of inspiration or a bout of melancholy, I turn to words to help me express it.

I constantly push myself to write clearly, concisely, and professionally. My background is in marketing, but I went back and got my master’s degree in communication. I wrote my thesis on keeping and sharing secrets. Why? Because come on! What a cool field of study?
